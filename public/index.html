— 01 / OUR WORK

What happens here.

Liberty Place isn't a treatment center. It's a home — a structured, supportive place where eight residents at a time rebuild the routines, relationships, and self-respect that addiction takes away.

01

Structure & Accountability

House meetings, curfews, chores, and weekly check-ins with our resident manager. The small things that make a big life possible again.

02

Work & Purpose

Every resident works or attends school within 30 days. We partner with local employers who know what second chances look like.

03

Community

Shared dinners. Sunday breakfast. Game nights. The things that replace the isolation recovery demands you leave behind.
